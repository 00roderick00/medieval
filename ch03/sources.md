## T2 — Canonical scholarship (open/bundled, consulted)

- **Howard Kaminsky, "The Noble Feud in the Later Middle Ages," *Past &
  Present* 177 (2002)** — open full-text PDF (FES mirror). The chapter's
  first-rank open medieval source: the feud as legal institution and
  alternative peace-system.
- **Charles Tilly, "War Making and State Making as Organized Crime" (1985)**
  — open PDF. Component support for the composite thesis.
- **McFate, *Durable Disorder*** (bundled) — the PMSC spine; the monopsony
  point. **Williams (2008)** (bundled) — the counter-read. **Cerny 2005**
  (bundled) — fifth/sixth characteristics. See corpus/bundled/index.md.
- **Roger Virgoe, "The Death of William de la Pole"** (Bull. John Rylands
  Library, open PDF) — 1450 context shared with ch07.
