# Brief — Ch. 4: The Condottieri (~11,000 words)
PURPOSE: The Italian system in full: Hawkwood and the compagnia di ventura
(Mallett); the condotta as legal instrument; domestication by market
competition, not state force; Sforza's mercenary-to-sovereign transition and
its enabling conditions. The Prigozhin question done properly: Sforza-from-
below now impossible; Sforza-from-above (Palantir, Anduril, frontier-lab
clearance dependencies) as the modern pattern. The Hansa as proto-platform
(Hanserecht, Kontors, fleets) — the better parallel than Church or Empire.
Venice as surveillance-state-before-the-state (Ten, bocche di leone,
Arsenal, prestiti). Close Part I: the literature sees fragmentation, not
the pattern that ends it.
RESEARCH FOCUS: Mallett, Dollinger, Lane (retrieval); Wagner/Prigozhin
reporting 2022–23; Palantir/Anduril procurement filings.
COMPARATORS: Italian states, the Hansa, Venice; Russia and the US bloc for
the modern layer.
OBJECTION (critiques.md): the Hansa-flattening complaint — structural not
functional parallel, conceded per outline.md.
